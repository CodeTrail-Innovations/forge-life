Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 3.8.2025

1. Introduction

Forge Life ("we," "our," or "us") provides a mobile application ("App") that allows users to generate statistics, store personal reflections (Future Letter), and manage tasks. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data in compliance with U.S. laws (CCPA), GDPR (if applicable), and Apple App Store guidelines.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and password (if applicable) when creating an account.
  • Image Data: Users can upload images of journal pages for statistical analysis and Future Letter. These are securely stored and deleted after one year.
  • App Usage Data: Anonymized analytics such as device type and app interactions help us improve our services.
  • Cookies & Tracking: Used for analytics and functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide statistical insights from OCR-processed journal pages.
  • To store and retrieve Future Letter images, deleted after one year.
  • To manage tasks and user-generated content.
  • To analyze app performance and enhance user experience.
  • To comply with legal requirements and prevent fraud.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. However, we may use third-party services for:

  • Cloud Storage: Google Firebase
  • Analytics: Apple App Analytics
  • Push Notifications: Firebase Cloud Messaging

These providers process your data under strict security measures.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Future Letter images are automatically deleted after one year.
  • You can request account and data deletion at any time by contacting us.

6. User Rights (CCPA & GDPR Compliance)

Depending on your location, you have rights such as:

  • Access to your data
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion of your personal data
  • Opting out of analytics tracking

California residents can exercise their CCPA rights by contacting us.

7. Data Security

We implement encryption, secure servers, and authentication controls to protect your personal data. However, no system is 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the revised policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@forge-life.com.
